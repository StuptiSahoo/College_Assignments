Dragoman:-

  1. A dragoman is an interpreter, translator, and official guide between countries that speak Arabic, Turkish, or Persian and European embassies, consulates, vice-consulates, and trading posts. Dragomans must have knowledge of Arabic, Persian, Turkish, and European languages.
Synonyms Antonyms
Linguist, Adapter, Cryptographer, Cryptologist, Decoder Interpreter, Translator
